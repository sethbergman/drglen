About V.I.P. Care

Total wellness

I promise to put your specific and individual needs first. I am known for providing high level, quality chiropractic services with VIP patient care. I am committed to your overall health and wellness.  I promise to give you and your family the best possible chiropractic experience. 

Dr. Glen Wilkinson D.C.

My journey to heal began at Texas State University where I received a bachelor's degree in Exercise and Sports Science. I continued my journey at Parker University in Dallas where I received my Doctorate of Chiropractic, and am now a licensed chiropractor in Austin Texas. In addition to chiropractic, I also carry degrees in Kinesiology, Anatomy, and Health and Wellness.

Treatment

Treatment begins by assessing your existing conditions. I also make it my mission to prevent pain and illness from reoccurring. The goal of our treatment is to help you improve your quality of life, achieve your wellness goals, and heal your body to live your best possible life.
